About Recruitmeco

Built for teams that hire at scale

Recruitmeco is an AI-powered hiring operations solution designed to help companies manage candidate volume with speed, structure and consistency.

We do not replace recruiters. We give them a cleaner, faster and more structured pipeline so they can focus on interviews, final decisions and human judgement.

Zurich-based. Serving hiring teams across Switzerland, Europe and Turkey.

🎯
Our Mission
To simplify hiring through clean candidate data, structured screening workflows and automated scheduling support.
🔭
Our Vision
To make recruitment operations faster, fairer and more efficient by helping teams focus on people instead of repetitive admin work.
🤝
Our Promise
Reduce recruiter workload, improve candidate filtering and make hiring operations easier without unnecessary software complexity.
