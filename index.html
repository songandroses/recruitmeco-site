Who we are

Recruitmeco is built for organisations that need a faster, cleaner and more scalable way to manage recruitment operations.

About Recruitmeco

Recruitmeco is an AI-powered hiring operations solution designed to help companies manage candidate volume with speed, structure and consistency.

Our Vision

To make recruitment operations faster, fairer and more efficient by helping teams focus on people instead of repetitive admin work.

Our Mission

To simplify hiring through clean candidate data, structured screening workflows and automated scheduling support.

A practical AI hiring operations layer for high-volume recruitment teams.

Recruitmeco helps companies manage the repetitive first layer of recruitment: candidate screening, qualification, scheduling and reporting. We do not replace recruiters; we give them a cleaner, faster and more structured pipeline so they can focus on interviews, final decisions and human judgement.

What we sell

An AI-supported recruitment operations service that turns unstructured applications into organised candidate shortlists, including screening forms, eligibility checks, fit scoring, interview coordination and weekly dashboards.

What clients can expect

Faster response times, fewer unsuitable candidates reaching recruiters, better visibility over applicant quality and a more consistent candidate experience across markets and roles.

Our promise

Reduce recruiter workload, improve candidate filtering and make hiring operations easier to manage without adding unnecessary software complexity.
